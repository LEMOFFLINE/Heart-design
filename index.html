居心空间创立于2017年，是一家专注于高端住宅与商业空间设计的先锋设计事务所。我们以"居心所向，空间所往"为核心理念， 将东方美学哲学与现代设计语言完美融合，为每一位客户打造超越期待的空间体验。 多年来，我们已为超过100,000位客户实现了理想人居梦想，与200+知名品牌建立深度合作关系， 作品屡获国际设计大奖，被业界誉为"空间美学的革新者"。

Founded in 2017, heart studio is a pioneering design studio specializing in high-end residential and commercial space design. Guided by our philosophy "Design Your Heart's Desire," we seamlessly blend Eastern aesthetic philosophy with modern design language, creating spatial experiences that exceed expectations for every client. Over the years, we have realized ideal living dreams for over 100,000 clients and established deep partnerships with 200+ renowned brands. Our works have won numerous international design awards, earning us the reputation of "Innovators of Spatial Aesthetics" in the industry.
